Hard Rock Hotel Maldives

Ocean Retreat & Spa offers excellent facilities for relaxation and recreation. Immerse yourself in the basics of local Maldivian culture and explore the fascinating island and the stories of the traditional guardians. Spend the day on the beach or explore the stunning reefs and lagoons that surround the island. Indulge your senses at the spa or enjoy local cuisine at the restaurant. Guraidhoo Island, where the hotel is located, is less developed than Maafushi, but more beautiful and quieter.

Term: 8 day/7 evening

Cost: 12 000 000 from soum
